Ragie developed an algorithmic approach to extract structured data from complex spreadsheets by treating them as graphs, identifying "islands" of related data using vectorized operations rather than processing individual cells. The approach lets Ragie handle messy, visually-laid-out spreadsheets at scale without excessive computational cost.
